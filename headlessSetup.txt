run:
sudo nano /etc/systemd/system/intruder_camera.service

paste this content into intruder_camera.service:
[Unit]
Description=IntruderNet Camera Capture
After=network.target

[Service]
User=mircea
WorkingDirectory=/home/mircea
ExecStart=/usr/bin/python3 /home/mircea/Desktop/IntruderNet/capture.py
Restart=always

[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target

run these commands at the end:
sudo systemctl daemon-reload
sudo systemctl enable intruder_camera.service
